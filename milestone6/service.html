Service is the heartbeat of The Grand Horizon. We take immense pride in offering a 24/7 concierge desk capable of handling any request, from securing hard-to-get theater tickets to arranging private helicopter tours of the coastline. If you require immediate assistance during your stay, please dial "0" from your in-room phone or call our main service line at +1 (555) 764-9000. Dining and Room Service: Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Elena Rossi, has crafted a menu that celebrates international flavors with a local twist. Room service is available 24 hours a day. Breakfast (6:00 AM - 11:00 AM): Enjoy our signature Truffle Eggs Benedict or an Acai Bowl with garden-fresh berries. All-Day Dining: Try the "Horizon Wagyu Burger" or our famous Lobster Linguine. Late Night: A curated selection of artisan pizzas and gourmet sandwiches is available from midnight until dawn. Wellness and Leisure: Beyond dining, we offer a full-service spa, "The Azure Retreat," featuring ten treatment rooms, a Himalayan salt sauna, and an infinity-edge hydrotherapy pool. Our fitness center is equipped with the latest cardio and weight-training machines, and we offer daily sunrise yoga sessions on the East Terrace. For business travelers, our Executive Lounge provides high-speed fiber internet, private meeting pods, and secretarial services. Whatever you need to make your stay more comfortable, our staff is trained to anticipate your needs before you even have to ask.
