Experience Coastal Elegance

Welcome to The Grand Horizon Hotel, where timeless elegance meets modern coastal luxury. Situated on a pristine stretch of ivory sand, our hotel has been the premier destination for discerning travelers for over a decade. We believe that a stay at a hotel should be more than just a place to sleep; it should be a transformative experience that restores the spirit and delights the senses.

Sophisticated Design & Hospitality

Our architecture blends classic Mediterranean influences with contemporary minimalist design, creating an atmosphere of "quiet luxury" that allows the natural beauty of the surrounding ocean to take center stage. From the moment you step into our vaulted marble lobby, you are treated with the dedicated "Horizon Hospitality" that has made us famous.

Whether you are visiting for a high-powered business conference, a romantic honeymoon, or a much-needed family retreat, our facilities are designed to cater to your every whim. We offer 250 guest rooms, including 40 oversized suites, each featuring floor-to-ceiling windows, private balconies, and smart-room technology that allows you to customize your lighting and climate with a single touch.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At The Grand Horizon, we are also committed to sustainable luxury. We utilize solar energy for 40% of our power needs and have a strict zero-plastic policy across all our dining venues. Our on-site gardens provide fresh herbs and vegetables for our world-class kitchen, ensuring that every meal is as fresh as the sea breeze.

Experience the perfect harmony of relaxation and sophistication. Your journey to the horizon starts here, and we cannot wait to welcome you home.
